Aegis GRC Platform
Aegis is a comprehensive Governance, Risk & Compliance platform designed for GRC Officers. It connects directly to security solutions like Active Directory, Okta, and Tenable to automate evidence collection, maps controls to NIST CSF 2.0, and tracks risks, findings, audits, policies, and vendors — all in one place.
